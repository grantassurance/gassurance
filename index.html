Our services

Regulatory Due Diligence

EUDR, UKTR, Lacey Act and Australian ILPR due diligence systems. Supplier onboarding, risk assessment, DDS procedures and TRACES workflow support for timber, coffee, palm, soy and other land-use commodities.

Risk Assessment & Sourcing Assurance

Certification, Auditing & Assurance

About Grant Assurance

Independent expertise,
delivered with integrity

Grant Assurance is an independent consultancy providing supply chain assurance, regulatory compliance, certification auditing and sustainable finance advisory services to operators, traders, buyers, investors and certification bodies across the globe.

With over 30 years of experience spanning forestry, land-use commodities and environmental compliance, our work draws on a career that has taken us from forest contracting in Scandinavia to senior roles at the World Resources Institute in Washington DC, forest investment management in Southeast Asia, and business development leadership in the certification sector.

We have held senior positions with leading international organisations working across Indonesia, China, Singapore, the USA and the UK — including establishing certification programmes, managing ESG compliance for forestry fund acquisitions, and leading due diligence for corporate investors. Our career also spans sustainable finance, including ESG due diligence, fund deployment and environmental and social compliance for forestry and land-use asset acquisitions on behalf of private equity and institutional investors.

We now provide independent advisory and assurance services to clients in the forestry and agriculture sectors, combining deep technical knowledge with practical, commercially aware delivery.
